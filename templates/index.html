What We Do?

Our Mission

Student Energy at University of Port Harcourt is aimed at educating and creating awareness on the importance of inter-disciplinary actions towards the alleviation of our dependency on crude-oil through the acquisition of knowledge of other energy sources, technical skills, policy restructuring and leadership responsibilities that will create a better world for us in the nearest future.

Our Vision

To see youths lead the way to a sustainable and more equitable energy future.
